The story ofSika-Design

Our passion for and understanding of rattan dates back decades.We have been around long enough to call ourselves Scandinavia’s oldest producer of rattan and wicker furniture.
The story of our company begins in 1942, when Ankjær Andreasen produced the first batch of baskets, lamps, flower tables and other smaller interior items made of rush and willow.
